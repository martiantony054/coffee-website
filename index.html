TYPES

Coffee Beans are the seeds of a fruit called coffee cherry. Coffee cherries grow on coffee trees from a genus of plants called coffea. There is a wide variety of species of cofee plants , ranging from shrubs to trees.

  • Types of beans
    • Arabica:This is the most popular type of coffee bean and originates from Ethiopia. It has a mild, flavorful taste.
    • Robusta:This type of coffee bean is more economical to grow because it is resistant to disease and can withstand a wider range of temperatures. It has a bitter taste.
  • Types of Roasts
    • Light roast:This roast has a lighter color and more acidity.
    • Medium roast: This is the most popular roast and has a balanced flavor.
    • Dark roast:This roast has a dark color and a strong, bitter flavor.
  • Grind
    • Grind size affects the taste of coffee.
    • A medium grind is most common for automatic drip coffee makers.
    • A fine grind is used for espresso.
    • A coarse grind is used in French presses.
  • Decaf coffee This type of coffee has had most of the caffeine removed.
